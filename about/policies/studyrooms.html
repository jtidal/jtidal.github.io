Rooms L534, L535, L536, L537, L538

  1. Current City Tech students, or students accompanied by faculty/staff, may reserve study rooms for academic purposes only.”
  2. Study rooms are available on a first come, first served basis.
  3. Reservations will be cancelled if a group arrives more than 15 minutes past their scheduled time.
  4. Study rooms may be used for up to 3 hours. There is a limit of 1 use per room per day for any group.
  5. Rooms L534 and L535 must have at least 3 students with a maximum of 5 students for use up to 180 minutes.
  6. Rooms L536, L537, & L538 must have at least 1, 3, 4, or 5 students (maximum) for use up to 180 minutes.
  7. To use a study room, ALL students in the group must present their City Tech IDs at the Circulation Desk. The study room key will be checked out to each student in the library’s online system.
  8. The study room key must be returned to the Circulation Desk on time to avoid late fees. The overdue fine for returning the key late is 2 cents minute, with a maximum fine of $10.
  9. When leaving the study room, the group must ensure the room is clean, the equipment is functioning, furniture is in order, and the door is locked.
  10. Absolutely no eating or drinking is allowed in the study rooms.
